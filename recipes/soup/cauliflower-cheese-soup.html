Cauliflower Cheese Soup

Cooking Method: Slow Cooker

Ingredients

  • 1 Head of Cauliflower
  • 1 Leek
  • Garlic
  • Pepper
  • 1 Box of Chicken Broth
  • Heavy Cream
  • Cheddar Cheese

Directions

  1. Cut up a head of cauliflower into big chunks
  2. Slice a leek
  3. Add 1 & 2 to the slow cooker
  4. Add Garlic & Pepper to slow cooker
  5. Add Box of Chicken Broth to Slow cooker
  6. Place lid on slow cooker and cook on low for 6-8 hours
  7. Stir in heavy cream and cheddar cheese
