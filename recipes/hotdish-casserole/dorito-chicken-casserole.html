Dorito Chicken Casserole

Cooking Method: Oven

Tags: American

Ingredients

  • 2 cups chicken breast (cooked and shredded)
  • 21 oz cream of chicken soup (2 cans)
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 10 oz rotel (drained)
  • 16 oz corn (drained)
  • 2 cups colby jack cheese (shredded)
  • 10 oz dorito chips (crushed)

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°
  2. Prepare a casserole dish with nonstick cooking spray and set aside
  3. In a mixing bowl, mix cooked & shredded chicken, cream of chicken soup, sour cream, drained rotel, drained corn, and 1 cup of shredded cheese.
  4. Once well combined, place mixed ingredients into casserole dish
  5. Top the casserole with the remaining 1 cup of cheese and the crushed doritos
  6. Bake at 350° for 15 minutes or until warm throughout and cheese is melted
