Top The Tater Copy Recipe

cooking method: blender

tags: dip

Ingredients

  • 1 8 ounce block of cream cheese at room temperature
  • 1 cup of sour cream
  • 2/3 cup chopped chives
  • 1 teaspoon fresh-squeezed lemon juice(or more to taste)
  • 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon garlic powder

Directions

  1. Add everything except chives to blender
  2. Blend until mixed and proper consistency
  3. Cut chives into small pieces
  4. Add chives to blend and mix without the blender
