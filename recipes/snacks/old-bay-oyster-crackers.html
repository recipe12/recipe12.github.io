Old Bay Oyster Crackers

Cook Method: Oven

Ingredients

  • 1 pkg oyster crackers
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tblsp old bay seasoning

Directions

  1. Preheat Oven to 250°
  2. Measure vegetable oil into glass measuring cup
  3. Mix old bay seasoning with vegetable oil
  4. Place crackers in large glass bowl
  5. Pour oil mixture over the crackers
  6. Mix well
  7. Place the crackers in single layer on baking sheet and bake at 250° for 10 minutes
  8. Use a spatula to turn over the crackers
  9. Bake an additional 10 minutes
