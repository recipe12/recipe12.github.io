Ranch and Dill Oyster Crackers

Cooking Method: Oven

Ingredients

  • 1 pkg oyster crackers
  • 1 pkg (1.6 ounce) Original Ranch Dressing or Dips Mix
  • 3/4 cup oil
  • 1 Tblsp dill weed
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder

Directions

  1. Mix dressing mix, oil, and dill weed
  2. Pour over crackers and toss until all crackers are covered with the mixture
  3. Place crackers on a sheet pan and bake at 250° for 10 minutes
  4. Stir and flip crackers over before baking an additional 10 minutes
