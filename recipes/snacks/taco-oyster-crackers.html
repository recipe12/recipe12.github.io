Taco Oyster Crackers

Cooking Method: Oven

Ingredients

  • 2 pkg oyster crackers
  • 3/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 package taco seasoning
  • 1 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 1/2 tsp chili powder

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 250°
  2. Measure vegetable oil into 2 cup glass measuring cup
  3. Add the taco seasoning, garlic powder, oregano, and chili powder
  4. Mix well
  5. Place the crackers in a large glass bowl and pour oil mixture over the crackers
  6. Toss the crackers with the mixture until the crackers are completely covered
  7. Bake at 250° for 10 minutes
  8. Flip crackers over with spatula
  9. Bake an additional 10 minutes
